About Me

I was born in Wurtzberg, Germany on October 5th 1991. Moved to Korea and lived there until I was in 5th grade. I then moved to Olympia, Washington and have lived there ever since. I am a younger brother to a loving older sister named Sarah. I played the trumpet and tenor saxophone throughout middle school and high school. My mother was quite disappointed when I decided not to continue playing in my adult years. I also enjoyed playing tennis throughout high school. Decided to go to college at Central Washington and graduated with a BS in Biology and minor in Chemistry. I did not end up working in that field which brings me to the UW BootCamp class which I could not be more excited for.

Some of the things I enjoy in my spare time are staying active and riding my bike when the weather is nice. On lazy Sunday's I may sit down to play some online video games although I don't imagine having very many of those days while taking this BootCamp course. Besides coding I am currently learning how to cook because who enjoys eating the same meals over and over again. One day I hope to work with a development team on mobile app devlopemnt or even video game devlopment if I can get to that level. Only time and a ton of effort will tell.
